About Vocal Reminder

Vocal Reminder is a demo UI built to showcase Bootstrap 5 patterns and component remixing. The goal is to present a clean, accessible interface that scales across devices.

Design Approach

We use Bootstrap utilities for spacing and layout, combine cards and hero patterns from the official examples, and add minimal custom CSS to keep the look modern and cohesive.

Tech Stack

  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • Bootstrap 5 (via CDN)
  • Static hosting (GitHub Pages / Netlify)

Services

Personal reminders
Record and schedule personal voice notes.
Team reminders
Share reminders across a team for better coordination.
Cross-device sync
Access reminders anywhere.
